Facilities & Services

Spacious Reading Halls

Comfortable, well-lit seating for over 200 students, creating a perfect environment for focused study.

Digital Library

Access thousands of e-books, e-journals, and premier databases like JSTOR and IEEE Open Library.

Easy Borrowing

Enjoy a streamlined and simple borrowing process for our extensive collection of physical books.

Digital Lab & Wi-Fi

Utilize our computers with high-speed internet or connect your own devices to the campus-wide Wi-Fi.

Research Assistance

Our expert librarians are available at assistance desks to guide you with research and academic writing.

Smart & Secure Access

Secure, ID-based entry for all university students and faculty, with cloakrooms available for safe storage.
